Dubai Love & Lights – 5 Days / 4 Nights

A romantic and illuminated escape designed for couples and dreamers who want to experience Dubai’s glowing nights, magical attractions, and Arabian charm.

Itinerary Highlights (Day-by-Day)

🕘 Day 1: Arrival & Marina Dhow Cruise Dinner

Begin your romantic escape with a luxury dinner cruise through the illuminated Dubai Marina.

🕘 Day 2: City Tour & Dubai Garden Glow

Explore Dubai’s cultural highlights by day and wander through the glowing sculptures of Garden Glow by night.

🕘 Day 3: Desert Safari & BBQ Dinner

Enjoy dune bashing, camel riding, sandboarding, and a starlit desert BBQ with live entertainment.

🕘 Day 4: Museum of the Future & Dubai Fountain

Discover futuristic innovation or shop at Dubai Mall before witnessing the world‑famous dancing fountain show.

🕘 Day 5: Final Departure

Relax with a final breakfast before your private transfer to the airport.
