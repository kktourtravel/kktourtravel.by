6-Day Zanzibar Beach Holiday & Mikumi Safari Adventure (2026)

Experience the perfect harmony of Zanzibar’s turquoise waters and Tanzania’s wild heart with this expertly curated 6-day, 5-night journey. From the aromatic spice plantations and historic alleys of Stone Town to the untamed savannahs of Mikumi National Park, this package is the ultimate East African escape.

Key Highlights

  • Stone Town Heritage: Guided exploration of UNESCO-listed sites and iconic Zanzibari architecture.
  • Spice Island Immersion: Discover vanilla, nutmeg, cloves, and tropical fruits.
  • Mikumi Fly-In Safari: Witness lions, elephants, giraffes, and rare African wild dogs.
  • Seaside Serenity: Relax on Zanzibar’s world-famous white-sand beaches.
  • Luxury & Convenience: All-inclusive transfers, flights, and professional guiding.

Day-by-Day Journey Overview

  • Day 1: Arrival in Zanzibar + beachfront dinner.
  • Day 2: Full leisure day on the coast.
  • Day 3: Stone Town tour + Spice Plantation experience.
  • Day 4: Fly to Mikumi for a full-day safari + hot buffet lunch.
  • Day 5: Leisure day + optional Sunset Dhow Cruise or Mnemba snorkeling.
  • Day 6: Final breakfast + private airport transfer.
