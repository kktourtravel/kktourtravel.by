Experience the Magic of Zanzibar

Indulge in the ultimate blend of historical charm and turquoise-water bliss. This curated 5-day Zanzibar Holiday takes you from the winding, aromatic alleys of Stone Town to the pristine white sands of the Indian Ocean. Whether you are snorkeling in vibrant coral reefs or savoring a spice-infused BBQ lunch on a secluded sandbank, this getaway is designed for those who seek both adventure and soulful relaxation.

Key Highlights

  • Stone Town & Spice Immersion – Explore the UNESCO World Heritage site.
  • Safari Blue – Snorkeling, dolphin spotting & seafood BBQ on a sandbank.
  • 4★ / 5★ Beachfront Resorts with private transfers.
  • Optimized schedule for maximum relaxation.

Itinerary Overview

  • Day 1: Arrival & Coastal Welcome – Private transfer to your beachfront hotel.
  • Day 2: Stone Town walking tour + Spice Farm visit.
  • Day 3: Full-day Safari Blue excursion.
  • Day 4: Free leisure day or optional village visit.
