Experience the Ultimate 14-Day East Africa Safari & Zanzibar Holiday

Embark on a breathtaking 14-day journey through Kenya, Uganda, and Zanzibar. Witness the iconic Big Five in the Maasai Mara, trek with chimpanzees in Uganda’s lush Kibaale Forest, cruise the wildlife-rich Kazinga Channel, and unwind on Zanzibar’s pristine beaches. This expertly curated adventure blends wildlife, culture, and tropical relaxation.

Key Highlights

  • Iconic Wildlife Safaris: Explore the Maasai Mara for lions, leopards, elephants, rhinos, and buffaloes.
  • Primate Encounters: Chimpanzee trekking in Uganda’s Kibaale Forest.
  • Scenic Natural Wonders: Boat cruise on the Kazinga Channel with the highest hippo concentration.
  • Cultural & Sensory Immersion: Stone Town exploration, spice farms, and swimming with sea turtles.
  • Ultimate Beach Relaxation: Multiple leisure days on Zanzibar’s white-sand beaches.

Detailed Itinerary

  • Day 1: Land in Nairobi and take a breathtaking scenic flight directly into the heart of the Maasai Mara for your first taste of the African wilderness.
  • Day 2: Dedicate an entire day to tracking the "Big Five"—lion, leopard, buffalo, rhino, and elephant—with expert guides in the iconic Maasai Mara.
  • Day 3: Explore hidden corners of the savannah during morning and evening game drives, perfectly timed for golden-hour photography and predator sightings.
  • Day 4: Return to Nairobi for an up-close encounter at the Giraffe Centre before catching an international flight to Kampala, Uganda.
  • Day 5: Settle into your Ugandan accommodation and enjoy a peaceful evening of dinner and preparation for your primate trekking adventure.
  • Day 6: Scenic Drive to Kibale Forest National Park – Travel through lush green hills to Kibale, home to 13 primate species and over 1,000 chimpanzees.
  • Day 7: Embark on a high-adrenaline chimpanzee trek in the morning, followed by a guided nature walk through the rich biodiversity of Bigodi Swamp.
  • Day 8: Transfer to Uganda’s premier park for a mix of crater lake exploration and an authentic cultural visit with the Basonghola community.
  • Day 9: Spot wildlife on the Kasenyi plains and take a sunset boat cruise on the Kazinga Channel, home to the world’s highest concentration of hippos.
  • Day 10: Leave the mainland behind and fly to the spice-scented, turquoise shores of Zanzibar to begin your luxury beach retreat.
  • Day 11: Walk through the 19th-century alleys of UNESCO-listed Stone Town and visit a spice farm to see where cloves and nutmeg are grown.
  • Day 12: Enjoy a day of pure relaxation on Zanzibar's world-renowned beaches, perfect for swimming or soaking up the Indian Ocean sun.
  • Day 13: Visit a unique natural lagoon in Nungwi for a rare and ethical opportunity to swim with majestic sea turtles.
  • Day 14: Enjoy one last morning on the beach before your private transfer to the airport for your journey home.
