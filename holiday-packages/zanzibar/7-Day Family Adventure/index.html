Experience the Ultimate 7-Day Zanzibar Family Holiday

Escape to the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean with our 7-Day, 6-Night Zanzibar Family Adventure. This comprehensive tour balances cultural immersion in the historic streets of Stone Town with serene beach relaxation and thrilling marine excursions. From encountering giant 200-year-old tortoises on Prison Island to savoring authentic Swahili flavors, this package is designed to create lifelong memories.

Key Highlights

  • Stone Town Heritage Tour – Explore iconic doors, museums, and historic alleys.
  • Prison Island Excursion – Meet giant tortoises & snorkel in coral reefs.
  • Authentic Swahili Cuisine – Lunch at Lukmaan & dinner at Forodhani Market.
  • Spice Farm Journey – Taste cloves, vanilla, nutmeg & tropical fruits.
  • Mnemba Atoll Snorkeling – Full-day marine adventure + sunset dhow cruise.
  • Beach Leisure – Relax on world-famous white-sand beaches.

Detailed Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival & Welcome – Private transfer to Stone Town hotel.
  • Day 2: Stone Town Culture & Street Food – Full-day tour + Forodhani dinner.
  • Day 3: Prison Island & Snorkeling – Half-day excursion + free afternoon.
  • Day 4: Spice Farm Picnic + Transfer to Beach Resort.
  • Day 5: Mnemba Island Snorkeling + Sunset Cruise.
  • Day 6: Full Beach Leisure Day.
  • Day 7: Departure – Private airport transfer.
